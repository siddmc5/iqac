Internal Quality Assurance Cell (Christ Deemed to be University)

The Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) of Christ (Deemed to be University) was established in 2001 as per the guidelines of the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). The IQAC works towards the realization of the goals of quality enhancement and sustenance.

IQAC adopts a participatory approach and seeks the cooperation of all the stakeholders to ensure the all-round development of the institution. The cell is committed to promoting a culture of quality in all aspects of the institution's functioning.

The IQAC also conducts quality initiatives in line with the guidelines of NAAC and other statutory bodies in accordance with the Vision and Mission of the University.

IQAC Objectives

  • Develop a system for conscious, consistent and catalytic improvement in the overall performance of the University.
  • Promote measures for institutional functioning towards quality enhancement through internalization of quality culture and institutionalization of best practices.
  • Ensure timely, efficient and progressive performance of academic and administrative tasks.
  • Facilitate the creation of a learner-centric environment conducive to quality education.
  • Arrange for feedback from students, parents and other stakeholders on quality-related institutional processes.
"Quality is never an accident; it is always the result of high intention, sincere effort, intelligent direction and skillful execution."
- IQAC, Christ (Deemed to be University)

Our Mission

To develop a system for conscious, consistent and catalytic action to improve the academic and administrative performance of the institution, and to promote measures for institutional functioning towards quality enhancement through internalization of quality culture and institutionalization of best practices.
